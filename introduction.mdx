## Welcome

Webump is a Discord promotion bot that helps grow your server by increasing visibility through easy-to-use commands. It offers free and premium features like auto bumping, custom banners, and embed colors for effective server promotion.

## Adding WeBump Bot

To add WeBump to your Discord server, simply click the link below and follow the prompts:

[Invite WeBump to Your Server](https://discord.com/oauth2/authorize?client_id=1154077045903593555)

Make sure you grant the necessary permissions for WeBump to function effectively.
